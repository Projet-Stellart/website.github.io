More about us

In a context where the quality of multiplayer experiences in games seems to be declining, our team has taken on the challenge of transforming and revitalizing the current landscape of video games. While Battle Royales and MOBAs have dominated in recent years, there is an opening for a renaissance of the FPS, the iconic genre that has influenced players so much.

The concept we propose is much more than just a team-based FPS, it takes place in the cosmic vastness, a mysterious and captivating environment where each session promises to be unique. One of our major innovations lies in the design of the map. Instead of having a fixed battlefield, we have opted for a map that changes with each game. This procedural approach offers unparalleled variety, with each match providing a new adventure, a different strategy to develop, and new challenges to overcome.

By choosing a futuristic and spatial setting, we can also introduce unprecedented gameplay mechanics, blending zero gravity or cutting-edge technologies. Genre enthusiasts, familiar with more classic mechanics, will discover a renewed experience while remaining rooted in the FPS's foundations.

This project is more than just a game; it is a bold exploration into the uncharted possibilities of FPS, a journey towards new gameplay horizons, and a surprise with each new session. It is our way of envisioning the future of interactive entertainment, combining tradition and modernity, comfort and discovery.
